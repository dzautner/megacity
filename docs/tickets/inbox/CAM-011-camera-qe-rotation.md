# CAM-011: Q/E Camera Rotation (90-Degree Snap)

## Priority: T1 (Core Polish)
## Effort: Small (0.5 day)
## Source: camera_controls_ux.md -- Section 14.2: Keybindings

## Description
Q and E keys rotate the camera 90 degrees left/right with smooth animation. Standard in RTS and city builders.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Q rotates camera 90 degrees counterclockwise
- [ ] E rotates camera 90 degrees clockwise
- [ ] Smooth animated rotation (not instant snap)
- [ ] Works alongside existing right-click orbit
