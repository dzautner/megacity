# END-025: Comprehensive Achievement Taxonomy

**Category:** Endgame / Progression
**Priority:** T3
**Source:** endgame_replayability.md -- Scoring and Achievement Systems

## Summary

Expand achievement system with 7 categories: Growth, Efficiency, Resilience, Creativity, Social, Environmental, Endgame, Challenge. Include "anti-achievements" for interesting failures (The Detroiter, The Enron, etc.). Progressive bronze/silver/gold tiers.

## Details

- Growth: population milestones, growth rate records, density achievements
- Efficiency: budget ratios, transit efficiency, energy efficiency, service coverage
- Resilience: survive disasters, recover from recession, maintain during crisis
- Creativity: no highways, 15-min city, linear city, symmetric city
- Social: low Gini, zero homelessness, 100% graduation, low crime
- Environmental: carbon neutral, 100% renewable, zero waste, restore polluted river
- Anti-achievements: population collapse, bankruptcy, environmental catastrophe

## Dependencies

- Achievement system (exists, needs expansion)
- All simulation metrics

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] 50+ achievements across 7 categories
- [ ] Anti-achievements with humorous presentation
- [ ] Progressive tiers (bronze/silver/gold)
- [ ] Achievement rewards (cosmetics, building variants)
