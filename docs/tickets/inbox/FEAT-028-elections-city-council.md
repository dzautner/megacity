# FEAT-028: Elections and City Council

**Category:** Feature / Governance
**Priority:** T3
**Source:** community_wishlists.md -- Section 10.1

## Summary

Mayor elections where citizens vote based on satisfaction. City council debating and voting on policies. Citizen protests disrupting operations. Public participation (town halls, hearings). Political factions (environmentalists, business, labor).

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Periodic elections with approval-based outcomes
- [ ] City council votes on major policies
- [ ] Citizen protests functional
- [ ] Public hearings for major developments
