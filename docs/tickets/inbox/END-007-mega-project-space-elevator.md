# END-007: Mega-Project -- Space Elevator

**Category:** Endgame / Mega-Project
**Priority:** T4
**Source:** endgame_replayability.md -- Mega-Projects as Endgame Goals

## Summary

Orbital tether providing cheap space access. Requires population > 500K, world-class university, extremely strong economy. 5 construction phases over 7-10 game years. Transforms city into global hub for space industry.

## Details

- Phases: R&D (2-3yr), anchor construction (1-2yr), cable deployment (2-3yr), climber systems (1yr), commercial operation
- Visually dramatic: thin line extending into sky, gradually thickening
- Effects: massive high-tech investment, space tourism, new economic sectors, world fame
- May trigger geopolitical events
- Area around anchor becomes prime real estate or security exclusion zone

## Dependencies

- Economy (massive funding)
- Education system (world-class university)
- END-003 (political consensus)

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Space elevator placeable with prerequisite checks
- [ ] Multi-year construction with visible phases
- [ ] Completed elevator creates new economic sectors
- [ ] Visual cable extending into sky
