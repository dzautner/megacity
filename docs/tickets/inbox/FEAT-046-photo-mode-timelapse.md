# FEAT-046: Photo Mode, Cinematic Camera, and Timelapse

**Category:** Feature / Player Expression
**Priority:** T3
**Source:** game_design_mechanics.md -- Section 3.6

## Summary

Photo mode with camera/lens controls (focal length, aperture, DOF), color grading, weather/atmosphere manipulation, filter presets. Timelapse recording of city growth from founding to megacity. Cinematic camera with keyframed paths, dolly/crane/orbit movements.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Photo mode with visual controls
- [ ] Filter presets (vintage, noir, cinematic)
- [ ] Timelapse recording as video/GIF
- [ ] Cinematic camera paths recordable
