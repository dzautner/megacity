# UX-062: District Selection and Inspection

## Priority: T2 (Depth)
## Effort: Small (1-2 days)
## Source: camera_controls_ux.md -- Section 10.1: Click-to-Select

## Description
Clicking a cell shows its district assignment. District info panel shows: name, population, jobs, happiness, services, and per-district statistics.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Cell click shows district name
- [ ] District info panel shows aggregate stats
- [ ] Population, jobs, happiness per district
- [ ] Service coverage per district
- [ ] District boundaries highlighted when district panel open
