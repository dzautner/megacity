# END-006: Mega-Project -- The Arcology

**Category:** Endgame / Mega-Project
**Priority:** T4
**Source:** endgame_replayability.md -- Mega-Projects as Endgame Goals

## Summary

Self-contained urban structure housing 50K-100K residents with integrated housing, commerce, services, energy, and food production. Multi-year construction with 6 phases. Requires population > 200K, advanced tech, high education, strong economy.

## Details

- 6 construction phases over 4-7 game years (planning, site prep, foundation, superstructure, interior, activation)
- Visible construction progress from across the city
- Prerequisites: 200K population, university campus, strong economy, political support
- Effects: houses 50K-100K in compact footprint, self-contained energy/water, integrated transit, tourist attraction
- Cost: 3-5 years of city budget (fundable via bonds, grants, private investment)
- New governance challenge: arcology residents develop distinct political identity

## Dependencies

- Building system (multi-cell construction)
- Economy (mega-project funding)
- END-003 (political support/opposition)

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Arcology placeable as multi-cell mega-project
- [ ] 6 construction phases with visible progress
- [ ] Prerequisites checked before construction allowed
- [ ] Completed arcology functions as self-contained city-within-city
