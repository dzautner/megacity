# SCEN-004: Car-Free City Thematic Challenge

**Category:** Scenario / Thematic
**Priority:** T4
**Source:** endgame_replayability.md -- Thematic Challenges

## Summary

No private car ownership within city limits. All transport via walking, cycling, or public transit. Must maintain economic viability, handle suburban commuters, provide emergency vehicle access.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Private car ban enforced
- [ ] Transit-only mode choice
- [ ] Economic viability maintained
