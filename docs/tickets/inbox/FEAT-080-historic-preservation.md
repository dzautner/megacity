# FEAT-080: Historic District Preservation

**Category:** Feature / Zoning
**Priority:** T3
**Source:** community_wishlists.md -- Section 2.3, 20.1

## Summary

Protect older buildings from demolition/redevelopment. Historic district designation prevents auto-upgrade and demolition. Tension between preservation and development. Historic buildings boost tourism and cultural value but constrain modernization.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Historic district designation tool
- [ ] Protected buildings cannot be auto-demolished
- [ ] Historic buildings boost tourism
- [ ] Player must choose preservation vs development
