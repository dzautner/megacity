# SCEN-005: Zero Homelessness Thematic Challenge

**Category:** Scenario / Thematic
**Priority:** T4
**Source:** endgame_replayability.md -- Thematic Challenges

## Summary

Every citizen has stable housing. No one spending more than 30% of income on housing. Adequate shelters/services for all vulnerable populations. Constraints: limited budget, political opposition to social housing.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Housing cost burden tracked per citizen
- [ ] Zero homelessness as win condition
- [ ] Opposition to social housing as constraint
