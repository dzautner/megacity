# FEAT-038: Adjustable Difficulty Settings

**Category:** Feature / Gameplay
**Priority:** T2
**Source:** community_wishlists.md -- Section 17 (VERY HIGH frequency)

## Summary

Multiple difficulty presets affecting starting money, growth speed, disaster frequency, service costs. Meaningful consequences for failures. Ironman/permadeath mode. Economic challenge (game should not become trivially easy). Realistic budget constraints forcing prioritization.

## Details

- Difficulty presets: Easy, Normal, Hard, Brutal
- Customizable modifiers: starting budget, growth rate, disaster frequency, maintenance costs
- Ironman mode: autosave-only, no reloading
- Budget designed to force tradeoffs at all stages
- Growth limits from geography, resources, infrastructure

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Difficulty settings selectable at game start
- [ ] At least 3 presets plus custom
- [ ] Ironman mode available
- [ ] Budget remains a challenge at large city sizes
