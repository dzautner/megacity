# UX-055: Budget Breakdown Panel

## Priority: T1 (Core Polish)
## Effort: Medium (2-3 days)
## Source: camera_controls_ux.md -- Section 13.2, master_architecture.md M2

## Description
Detailed budget panel showing separate income lines (tax by zone type, commercial, industrial) and expense lines (per service type, maintenance, utilities, loan payments). Net income prominently displayed.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Income breakdown: residential tax, commercial tax, industrial tax
- [ ] Expense breakdown: per service type, road maintenance, utility costs
- [ ] Net income: income - expenses shown prominently
- [ ] Monthly and yearly view toggle
- [ ] Slider controls for adjustable budget items
