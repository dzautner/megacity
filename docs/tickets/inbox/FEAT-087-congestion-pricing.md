# FEAT-087: Congestion Pricing and Toll Roads

**Category:** Feature / Transportation
**Priority:** T3
**Source:** endgame_replayability.md -- Congestion Ceiling

## Summary

Congestion pricing zones where drivers pay to enter during peak hours. Toll roads/bridges for revenue and demand management. Revenue from congestion pricing funds transit improvements. Variable pricing by time of day.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Congestion pricing zones designable
- [ ] Revenue generated from pricing
- [ ] Traffic reduction in priced zones
- [ ] Time-of-day variable pricing
