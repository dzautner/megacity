# SCEN-003: Carbon-Neutral City Thematic Challenge

**Category:** Scenario / Thematic
**Priority:** T4
**Source:** endgame_replayability.md -- Thematic Challenges

## Summary

Achieve net-zero carbon emissions. All energy from renewables. All transport electric or human-powered. All buildings passive house standard. Constraints: no fossil fuel power, no highway construction.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Carbon emissions tracked
- [ ] Renewable-only energy constraint enforced
- [ ] Win condition: net-zero emissions
