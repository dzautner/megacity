# FEAT-054: Named Citizens and Emergent Story Generation

**Category:** Feature / Narrative
**Priority:** T3
**Source:** game_design_mechanics.md -- Section 6.4

## Summary

Each citizen has name, age, family, occupation, home, workplace, happiness factors. Life events: birth, education, first job, marriage, children, retirement, death. Personality traits affecting behavior. Citizens remember events ("survived the great flood"). Story generation from life events creating emergent narratives.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Citizens have names, ages, families
- [ ] Life events tracked and displayed
- [ ] Personality traits affect behavior
- [ ] Citizen info panel shows life history
- [ ] Notable citizen stories surfaced in news feed
