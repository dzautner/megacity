# FEAT-091: Transit-Oriented Development Bonuses

**Category:** Feature / Zoning
**Priority:** T2
**Source:** community_wishlists.md -- Section 2.3

## Summary

Automatic density bonus near transit stations. Higher-density zoning allowed within walking distance of transit. Mixed-use encouraged near stations. Reduced parking requirements near transit. Creates natural growth around transit hubs.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Density bonus within transit station radius
- [ ] Higher zone level allowed near transit
- [ ] Parking requirement reduction near transit
- [ ] Visual density gradient around stations
