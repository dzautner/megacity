# PROG-009: Hope and Discontent Dual Meters (Frostpunk inspired)

**Category:** Progression / Governance
**Priority:** T3
**Source:** indie_competitors.md -- Frostpunk

## Summary

Dual meter system representing citizen optimism (Hope) and frustration (Discontent). Every decision affects one or both. Hope dropping too low or Discontent too high triggers political crisis. Moral decision framework with genuine dilemmas.

## Details

- Hope: represents citizen belief in city's future
- Discontent: represents anger at leadership
- Interact as opposing forces
- Decision events explicitly trade between them
- End-game review of all decisions made (moral scorecard)

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Hope and Discontent meters tracked
- [ ] Player decisions affect both meters
- [ ] Crisis triggered at extreme values
- [ ] Decision review/scorecard at milestones
