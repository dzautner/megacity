# FEAT-043: District Theming and Street Furniture

**Category:** Feature / Player Expression
**Priority:** T3
**Source:** game_design_mechanics.md -- Section 3.2

## Summary

District style system: assign architectural theme per district. Theme packs (Old Town, Business District, Arts Quarter, Waterfront). Street furniture per style (benches, lampposts, planters). Road surface options (cobblestone, asphalt, brick). Landscaping presets per district.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] District style assignable per district
- [ ] At least 4 theme packs
- [ ] Street furniture spawns per theme
- [ ] Road surface options per district
