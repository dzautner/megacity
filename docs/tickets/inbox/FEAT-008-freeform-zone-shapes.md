# FEAT-008: Non-Grid / Freeform Zone Shapes

**Category:** Feature / Zoning
**Priority:** T2
**Source:** community_wishlists.md -- Section 2.2 (VERY HIGH frequency)

## Summary

Polygonal/freeform zone shapes conforming to curved roads, not just rectangular grid squares. Larger zone depths. Corner buildings wrapping around street corners. Cul-de-sac support. Setback and minimum lot size controls.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Zones conform to road curves
- [ ] Variable zone depth setting
- [ ] Corner buildings at intersections
- [ ] Setback controls per district
