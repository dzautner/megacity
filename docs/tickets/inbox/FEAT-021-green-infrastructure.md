# FEAT-021: Green Infrastructure

**Category:** Feature / Environment
**Priority:** T3
**Source:** community_wishlists.md -- Section 6.3

## Summary

Green roofs/walls for insulation and stormwater management. Urban farming (community gardens, vertical farms). Renewable energy mix with realistic intermittency. Urban tree canopy providing shade, air quality, noise reduction, beauty.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Green roof option for buildings
- [ ] Urban farming zones/buildings
- [ ] Tree canopy effects on microclimate
- [ ] Renewable energy intermittency modeled
