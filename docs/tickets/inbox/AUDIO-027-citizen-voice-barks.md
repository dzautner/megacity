# AUDIO-027: Citizen Voice Barks at Close Zoom

**Category:** Audio / UX
**Priority:** T5
**Source:** game_design_mechanics.md -- Section 10.5 (Citizen Feedback)

## Summary

When zoomed in close to citizens, hear occasional voice barks. Happy citizens: humming, chatting, laughing. Unhappy citizens: grumbling, sighing, complaining. Context-specific: construction workers shouting, children playing, traffic honking. Only active at near camera distances.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Voice barks play at close zoom
- [ ] Bark type reflects citizen happiness
- [ ] Context-specific barks near relevant buildings
- [ ] Volume and frequency limited to prevent annoyance
