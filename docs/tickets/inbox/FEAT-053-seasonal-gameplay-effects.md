# FEAT-053: Seasonal Gameplay Effects

**Category:** Feature / Simulation
**Priority:** T2
**Source:** game_design_mechanics.md -- Section 6.2

## Summary

Spring: increased construction speed, parks bloom, tourism uptick. Summer: higher water/AC demand, heat waves, outdoor events. Autumn: harvest festivals, school year, foliage tourism. Winter: heating demand spike, snow removal, icy roads, holiday events, blizzards. Seasonal infrastructure: road maintenance in winter, solar efficiency, crop seasons.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Each season has distinct gameplay effects
- [ ] Heating/cooling demand varies by season
- [ ] Road maintenance increases in winter
- [ ] Seasonal visual changes (snow, foliage)
- [ ] Seasonal events and tourism patterns
