# FEAT-090: Insurance and Risk Management System

**Category:** Feature / Economy
**Priority:** T3
**Source:** endgame_replayability.md -- Environmental Debt

## Summary

Property insurance premiums based on flood, earthquake, fire risk. Insurance costs rise in high-risk areas. Insurance payouts after disasters reduce rebuild costs. Uninsured properties are total losses. Risk mitigation (flood walls, fire codes) reduces premiums.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Insurance premiums per building based on risk
- [ ] Payouts after disasters
- [ ] Risk mitigation reduces premiums
- [ ] Uninsured properties receive no disaster aid
