# UX-042: Highway Interchange Templates

## Priority: T4 (Polish)
## Effort: Medium (3-5 days)
## Source: master_architecture.md T4

## Description
Pre-built highway interchange designs (cloverleaf, diamond, trumpet, stack) that can be placed as a single unit. Reduces complexity of highway construction.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] At least 4 interchange templates: cloverleaf, diamond, trumpet, roundabout
- [ ] Preview during placement
- [ ] Auto-connects to adjacent roads
- [ ] Rotation support
- [ ] Cost calculated from component roads
