# AUDIO-022: Population Tier Music Sets

**Category:** Audio / Music
**Priority:** T4
**Source:** sound_design.md -- Section 3.4

## Summary

Different music compositions per population tier. Village (0-1K): sparse, intimate, solo instruments. Town (1K-10K): growing, hopeful, small ensemble. City (10K-50K): established, confident, full ensemble. Metropolis (50K-200K): grand, complex, orchestral. Megacity (200K+): epic, overwhelming, full orchestration. Transitions crossfade between tier music sets.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] At least 2 music pieces per population tier
- [ ] Arrangements grow in complexity with population
- [ ] Smooth crossfade between tier transitions
- [ ] Musical identity changes with city growth
