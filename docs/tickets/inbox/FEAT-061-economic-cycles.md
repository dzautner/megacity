# FEAT-061: Economic Cycles (Boom/Bust)

**Category:** Feature / Economy
**Priority:** T3
**Source:** master_architecture.md, endgame_replayability.md

## Summary

Economic cycles with 7-10 year periodicity: expansion, peak, contraction, trough. Semi-random timing and severity. City diversification affects recession resilience. Labor market with wage determination based on supply/demand. Inflation modeling.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Economic cycles create periodic revenue variation
- [ ] Recession reduces tax revenue and increases unemployment
- [ ] City diversification reduces recession severity
- [ ] Inflation affects prices over long game time
