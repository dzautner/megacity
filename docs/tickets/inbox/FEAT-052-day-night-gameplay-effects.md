# FEAT-052: Day/Night Cycle Gameplay Effects

**Category:** Feature / Simulation
**Priority:** T2
**Source:** game_design_mechanics.md -- Section 6.1

## Summary

Nighttime: reduced traffic, increased crime, nightlife activates, power demand shifts. Morning rush: traffic spike, school zones. Evening: commercial peak, entertainment demand. Night workers at hospitals, police, fire. Visual: streetlights, building glow, vehicle headlights.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Traffic patterns follow time-of-day
- [ ] Crime rate increases at night
- [ ] Power demand shifts between day/night
- [ ] Visual day/night effects (lights, glow)
