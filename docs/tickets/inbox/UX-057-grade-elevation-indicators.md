# UX-057: Road Grade and Elevation Indicators

## Priority: T3 (Future)
## Effort: Small (1-2 days)
## Source: camera_controls_ux.md -- Section 12.5: Grade and Elevation

## Description
During road placement on terrain with elevation, show elevation markers and grade coloring: green 0-3%, yellow 3-6%, red 6%+. Bridge/tunnel indicators where road crosses water or terrain.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Elevation numbers at regular intervals along road preview
- [ ] Grade color coding: green/yellow/red
- [ ] Bridge indicator where road crosses water
- [ ] Tunnel indicator where road goes through hill
