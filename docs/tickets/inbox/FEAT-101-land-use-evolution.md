# FEAT-101: Organic Land Use Evolution

**Category:** Feature / Zoning
**Priority:** T3
**Source:** community_wishlists.md -- Section 20.2

## Summary

Areas naturally transition from one use to another over time. Residential -> commercial as area densifies. Industrial -> commercial as industry moves to outskirts. Walking-first transport for short trips. Neighborhoods develop outward from activity centers.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Zone transitions happen organically based on conditions
- [ ] Residential areas develop commercial ground floors
- [ ] Industrial areas convert to commercial/residential over time
- [ ] Walking is default for short distances
