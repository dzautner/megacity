# END-013: Scenario -- The Megacity (Extreme Density)

**Category:** Endgame / Scenario
**Priority:** T4
**Source:** endgame_replayability.md -- Scenario and Challenge Modes

## Summary

Tokyo/Mumbai analog: 2M population on small land area with extreme density, traffic, housing costs, aging population, earthquake risk. Objectives: reduce commute time, increase housing affordability, prepare earthquake resilience.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Pre-built ultra-dense city with capacity challenges
- [ ] Transit system at capacity requiring expansion
- [ ] Earthquake preparedness as objective
