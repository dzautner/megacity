# FEAT-103: Signature and Landmark Buildings

**Category:** Feature / Buildings
**Priority:** T3
**Source:** game_design_mechanics.md -- Section 1.6

## Summary

Unique one-per-city buildings with powerful effects and dramatic visuals. Require specific conditions (95% happiness + 100K pop). Area-of-effect bonuses. Visual city centerpieces. Secret/hidden unlocks through exploration or specific build patterns.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] At least 5 landmark buildings
- [ ] Each has unique prerequisite conditions
- [ ] Area-of-effect bonuses from landmarks
- [ ] One-per-city limit
- [ ] Dramatic visual presence
