# END-012: Scenario -- The Sinking City

**Category:** Endgame / Scenario
**Priority:** T4
**Source:** endgame_replayability.md -- Scenario and Challenge Modes

## Summary

Coastal city scenario (Venice/New Orleans analog). 150K population on low-lying land with increasing flood frequency, historic center vulnerability, tourism-dependent economy, rising insurance costs. Objectives: protect historic center, maintain tourism, prepare for sea level rise.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Flooding mechanics functional on low-lying terrain
- [ ] Multiple flood mitigation strategies available (sea walls, managed retreat, Dutch-style)
- [ ] Win/loss conditions tracked
