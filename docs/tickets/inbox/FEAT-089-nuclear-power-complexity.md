# FEAT-089: Nuclear Power Plant Complexity

**Category:** Feature / Infrastructure
**Priority:** T3
**Source:** community_wishlists.md -- Section 5.2

## Summary

Nuclear power as high-capacity but complex option. Uranium supply chain. Waste disposal challenge (long-term storage). Meltdown risk from poor maintenance or disasters. High political opposition (NIMBY).

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Nuclear plant with high output
- [ ] Waste disposal requirement
- [ ] Meltdown risk from neglect
- [ ] Political opposition mechanic
