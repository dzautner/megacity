# AUDIO-026: District-Based Musical Identity

**Category:** Audio / Music
**Priority:** T5
**Source:** sound_design.md -- Section 3.9 (Anno 1800 lessons)

## Summary

Different districts or zone concentrations get subtle musical identity shifts. Camera over industrial area: heavier percussion, lower register. Over parks/residential: warmer, more melodic. Over commercial downtown: busier, more rhythmic. Implemented as filter/mix adjustments on existing stems based on dominant zone under camera.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Musical character shifts subtly based on camera position
- [ ] Industrial areas feel heavier
- [ ] Park/residential areas feel warmer
- [ ] Transitions smooth when panning across zones
