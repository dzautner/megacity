# AUDIO-004: Construction Site Audio

**Category:** Audio / Spatial
**Priority:** T4
**Source:** sound_design.md -- Section 2.3

## Summary

Construction sites (buildings under construction) emit construction sounds: hammering, drilling, heavy equipment, metal clanging, backup beeper. Triggered when buildings are in construction state. Only active during working hours (7am-6pm).

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Construction sounds play near buildings under construction
- [ ] Sounds only during working hours
- [ ] Volume scales with construction size
- [ ] Distance attenuation from camera
