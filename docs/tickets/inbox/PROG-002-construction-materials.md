# PROG-002: Construction Material Requirements

**Category:** Progression / Building
**Priority:** T3
**Source:** indie_competitors.md -- Workers & Resources

## Summary

Buildings require actual construction materials delivered to site. Construction offices dispatch workers and vehicles. Road/rail/infrastructure construction requires equipment and crew. Not instant placement.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Buildings require materials for construction
- [ ] Construction vehicles/workers dispatch to site
- [ ] Material delivery delay creates construction timeline
- [ ] Resource availability affects construction speed
