# FEAT-015: Multi-Tier Education Pipeline

**Category:** Feature / Services
**Priority:** T2
**Source:** community_wishlists.md -- Section 4.3

## Summary

Elementary, secondary, university, vocational training pipeline. Education affects job eligibility and salary. Brain drain (educated citizens leave if no appropriate jobs). Smaller school buildings for neighborhoods. Graduation rates and capacity limits per school.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] 4-tier education: elementary, secondary, university, vocational
- [ ] Education level affects job eligibility
- [ ] Brain drain if jobs don't match education
- [ ] School capacity limits enforced
