# FEAT-047: City Sharing and Community Gallery

**Category:** Feature / Social
**Priority:** T4
**Source:** game_design_mechanics.md -- Section 4.1, 4.4

## Summary

City export/import for sharing saves. City showcase gallery with screenshots, stats, description. City tours (load others' cities as read-only). City ratings on aesthetics/efficiency/creativity. Blueprint marketplace. Community events (monthly themes with special achievements).

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] City export/import functional
- [ ] Gallery with screenshots and stats
- [ ] Read-only city touring
- [ ] Blueprint sharing
