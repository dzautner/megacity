# FEAT-012: Housing Market and Real Estate Simulation

**Category:** Feature / Economy
**Priority:** T2
**Source:** community_wishlists.md -- Section 3.3

## Summary

Dynamic land/property values responding to amenities, transit, views, noise, pollution. Supply/demand housing market affecting rent and affordability. Gentrification mechanics. Real estate developers responding to market conditions. Rent control policy.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Property values respond to proximity factors
- [ ] Housing supply/demand affects rent
- [ ] Gentrification displaces lower-income residents
- [ ] Rent control policy option available
