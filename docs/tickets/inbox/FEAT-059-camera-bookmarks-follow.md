# FEAT-059: Camera Bookmarks and Follow Mode

**Category:** Feature / Camera
**Priority:** T2
**Source:** master_architecture.md -- Camera/Controls section

## Summary

Save and recall camera positions (bookmarks). Follow mode to track a citizen, vehicle, or service vehicle. Smooth camera interpolation (exponential lerp, frame-rate independent). Dynamic pitch limits based on zoom.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Camera bookmarks (save/load positions)
- [ ] Follow mode for citizens/vehicles
- [ ] Smooth interpolation between positions
- [ ] Dynamic pitch limits based on zoom level
