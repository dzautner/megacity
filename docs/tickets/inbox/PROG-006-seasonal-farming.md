# PROG-006: Seasonal Farming and Agriculture

**Category:** Progression / Economy
**Priority:** T4
**Source:** indie_competitors.md -- Ostriv, Banished

## Summary

Growing season with timing-dependent yields. Crop rotation with soil nutrient system. Livestock integration (cattle, chickens). Harvest must complete before winter or crops lost. Community gardens as urban agriculture option.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Crops follow seasonal growing cycle
- [ ] Late sowing reduces yield
- [ ] Crop rotation affects soil quality
- [ ] Urban farming as zone/building option
